This Cookies Policy explains how HotSinglesChat uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help remember your preferences and keep you logged in.

2. How we use cookies

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in between page loads.
  • Preferences: To remember settings like theme or age confirmation.
  • Analytics: To understand usage trends and improve the service (non-identifying data).

3. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit functionality (e.g., login persistence may not work).

At this time, we do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. If this changes, the Cookies Policy will be updated.

5. Updates
